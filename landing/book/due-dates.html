4 Due Dates

Because this is an online course, and it is entirely feasible that one might complete all of the work in short order, the due dates for work are necessarily extended.

4.1 Final Project and associated paradata

Submit the link to your final project by the final day of term, via a direct message to me in Slack. + I would also like a one-page summary of work to date, topic, any agreed upon collaboration (which would include a paragraph on roles, responsibilities, and plan for resolving disputes), scale and scope to be sent to me via direct message in Slack by the end of the first week of March.

4.2 Exhibit of your learning

That is, your selected module exercises. Submit the link to your exhibit detailing your growth in this classs, at any point up to the final day of term. While I have specified a minimum for what needs to go in here, you are free to include other items (such as blog posts, videos) etc that help tell the story.

The Assessment and Final Project pages should be examined carefully. Please contact Dr. Graham for any clarifications.
